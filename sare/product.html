The Sare Feeds Feeding Plan

Sare Millers feeds are nutritionally balanced feeds containing vitamins and trace minerals for cattle, pigs, fish and poultry. The process of feeding animals largely depends on the knowledge and information farmers have on the economic benefits he derives from using a particular type of feed. The most important one being the fact that any feed the farmer uses should lead to proper productivity and better yields. To achieve this, Sare feeds are highly prepared through a thorough process of technical science and balancing of formulation with a vision of providing affordable and quality animal feeds to farmers with the sole aim of improving agribusiness in the society particularly the women and youth. At Sare Millers, we factor in the economical impact of prices. Beneath is a broad outline that provides the farmer with a range of Sare feeds to help in deciding the right type of feed on the various stages of growth.

Our Products

CATTLE FEEDS

Sare Feeds Dairy Meal

This Feed is specifically meant to support milk production. It is properly balanced with all the necessary ingredients including yeasacc (a highly recommended yeast substance) to boost enzyme generation that in turn leads to an improved daily yield in milk). Molasses are used in content of 10% per ton of the entire formulation; making the feed more palatable to the dairy cows as well as improves on the quality of the product. At Sare Millers we strongly advice farmers not to put additional molasses since the Dairy Meal is already formulated with the molasses and this is aimed at reducing the feeding cost associated with the cow. The recommended feeding program for farmers should be 2kgs per 3.5 litres of milk produced or as some may prefer 4kgs per day. The Sare feed Dairy Meal is also fed to the in calf cow from 2 months before calving and continued till the lactation is complete & it is packed in 70, 50, 20 and 10 kg bags and sold according to the preference of the farmer.

High Yield Dairy Meal

As the name suggests, the Sare Feed High Yield Dairy Meal is meant to increase the yield in milk from normal standard to the highest quantity, it is properly formulated with very high protein content level, molasses is added and yeasacc used for enzyme purposes. Farmers who intend to yield highest from their cows are advised to use this brand of Sare Feed High Yield Dairy Meal. The Sare Feed High Yield Dairy Meal is also recommended for those farmers whose cows are yielding lower than the expected daily output of milk. It offers an immediate improvement on the dairy cow due to its intelligently balanced formula. Though slightly expensive than the normal Sare feed dairy meal, the high yield dairy meal offers an easier solution to the milk yield problems. It is best used during the lactation period of the cow. The high yield dairy meal should be fed to the cow at a rate of 2kgs per 4 litres of milk produced.

PIG FEEDS

Sow & Weaner Meal

Is a well balanced pig feed designed to feed pregnant sows and piglets up to 9 weeks of age. During the pregnancy it is administered at the rate of 2kg to 3kg per day; higher quantities being required during the 1st and last months of pregnancy. The feed is also ideal for the production of porkers. Piglets to be reared as porkers should be fed on Sow & Weaner Meal from 9 weeks of age till they reach 55kgs live weight and are ready for slaughter. The quantity of feed should not be restricted and ample water must be available at all times.

Pig Finishing Meal

Once the porker weight of 55kgs has been attained, the pigs are fed on Pig Finishing Meal till they reach the bacon weight of 80 to 90 kg. The feeding rate should be restricted to between 2kgs to 2.5kgs per day to ensure some economic value is got from the feed; the quantity being increased with increase in weight.

FISH FEEDS

Fish Starter

This fish feed is properly formulated to ensure very high protein content of up to 46% crude protein. The feed has premix and other trace element minerals to ensure faster growth of the fish. At Sare Millers we provide both mash and pellet form of the feed. We recommend the fish pellets due to their excellent FCR (the Food Conversion Ratio) as compared to the Mash. The choice is left for the farmer depending on the economic status of the farmer.

Fish Finisher

This fish feed is properly formulated for fish 9 weeks old and above. It’s meant to increase the weight of the fish ready for harvesting. Again the feed is supplied in both mash and pellets form. The feed has a crude protein content of 26%. The feed is given to the fish until the time of harvesting. It ensures the fish gains enough weight within the recommended time frame. At the time of harvesting the fish should be weighing more than 1kg at the 7th month. The feeding program should be 2kgs in the morning and 2kgs in the evening; of course with the observance of the key feeding parameters like the oxygen level in the water etc.

POULTRY FEEDS

Chick Mash

This is a carefully balanced feed suitable for feeding replacement chicks from a day old up to 8 weeks of age. It should be fed at liberty. It always contains coccidiostat to ensure protection of the chicks from coccidiosis.

Growers Mash

This is offered freely to the growing pullets from 8 weeks up to point of lay. It also contains coccidiostat at appropriate levels to build up immunity in the chicks. This feed is ensures the chicks grow at a faster rate and mature early enough to start laying eggs.

Layers Mash

Offered to birds during the laying period.Specially modified to fulfill various requirements. Should be fed at the rate of about 120 grams per day. Its protein, energy and vitamin trace mineral contents are higher so that even at low feed consumption rates, sufficient nutrients are ingested by the birds thus sustaining a high rate of egg production.

Kienyeji Mash

This is a specially formulated feed for the local poultry to ensure faster growth of the birds and early maturity. With improved local birds feeding accounts for the sizeable part of the enhancement of the birds laying process. The feeds are formulated with coccidiostat to ensure zero coccidiosis is reported by the farmer. The feeds have shown very encouraging results from the farmers who have used the feed in their farms..
